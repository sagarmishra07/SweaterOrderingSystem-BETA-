StoryBoarding

Users browses the url of webpage and  view the available sweaters in the organization with their specification and prices.
Then users place an order for sweaters by filling up the form and submit it by clicking submit button.Form includes fields like the
organization name,sizes,colours,quantities,delivery date,email,phone etc.They can give feedback using contact us page.Then admin login 
by using username and password and views order details,update details,delete details. Admin can view the history of the orders by searching 
the name of ordering organization or shop.
